Actors

Convener

Table 1. 

ActorConvener

The leader of the Rotary team responsible for the running of the Martinborough fair. Regular user, used to GUIs but will not be familiar with any particular software function. Impatient.


Stallholder

Table 2. 

ActorStallholder

A person or organisation running a stall at the Martinborough Fair. Normally undrestands the process including legal requirements in regard Health & Safety food. May have difficulty with systems, remembering passwords


SWDC

Table 3. 

ActorSWDC

The staff member or department of the South Wairarapa District Council responsible for approving stall food preparation and selling licences. Have their own process to follow, maybe reluctant to complete process unique to the system.


Public

Table 4. 

ActorPublic

Person attending the fair, will access the application for information, maps etc. Not expected to operate a GUI with subtlety or ease. May have diffivulty reading, short sighted colour blind.


Lions

Table 5. 

ActorLions

Voluntary organisation that supplies trestles to stallholders. Don't have to access the system other than the receiving the number of trestles need for the fair.
